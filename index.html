The Camps

Summer Fun Nationals Camp

This is a Masters, family friendly camp geared to prepare athletes for Summer Fun Nationals the end of the week. Younger athletes must have the ability to comfortably ski on hard snow conditions and negotiate getting from the lodge to the lane on their own.

July 6th–10th
Camp Price $920
Day Price $200
Official Wesbite summerfunnationals.com
Tickets Included

Pick up your ticket at 6:30 by Smokey the Bear (on the stairs to the second floor of the day lodge). Meet out on the hill by 7:30. Look for our lane under Willy Camp on the white board at the top of Palmer. Training will continue until 11:00 or until conditions deteriorate. Bring your own lunch, water and sunscreen.

High School GS & SL Camps

Open to high school and middle school athletes. Camps are specifically tailored to the the high school athlete and are designed to reinforce ski racing fundamentals. Advanced camps are meant to continue skill development from previous week.
